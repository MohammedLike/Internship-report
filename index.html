Overview

Executive Summary

During this internship at Stockwiz Technologies LLP, I contributed across the full spectrum of FinTech product development — from hands-on quantitative analysis of Nifty Pharma stocks and KPI benchmarking, to building Pine Script trading strategies and a full-stack automated report generation platform serving 2,370+ equities.

I engineered large-scale data pipelines scraping 2,000–3,370 listed stocks, researched competitive FinTech platforms and options-selling strategies, and prototyped an AI-based strategy generation system using local LLMs, RAG architecture, and MCP server integrations. Daily market intelligence reporting, backtesting validation, and scalping mode feature design rounded out a breadth of deliverables spanning research, engineering, and product thinking.
